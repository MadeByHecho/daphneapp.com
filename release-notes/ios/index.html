[Fix] A crash that would happen when your reminder was too short

[Fix] Daphne was only finding tasks you wanted to recur everyday if you spelled it all as one word, now you can spell it every day as well

Monday, June 6, 2016

[Add] Native iPad support

[Add] The ability to create recurring reminders like 'every November 16th', 'everyday at 9 PM'

[Add] The ability to snooze reminders

[Add] The ability to report reminders that didn't come out like you expected

[Add] The ability to snooze or complete reminders from the notification center

[Add] Support for reminders around American holidays
